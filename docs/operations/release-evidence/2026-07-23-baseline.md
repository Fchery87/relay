## Known open risks

- `convex-local-backend --instance-secret` has no environment-variable
  alternative in the installed binary's CLI; the secret is visible via
  `ps`/`/proc/<pid>/cmdline` to other local processes under the same user
  for the backend's lifetime. Accepted for a single-user local-dev machine;
  do not run this topology on a shared host. See
  [self-hosted-convex.md](../self-hosted-convex.md) for the mitigations
  considered and why they don't close this.
- Secret redaction in logs (`apps/daemon/src/observability/logger.ts`) is
  tested for known patterns but has not been audited across every
  log/diagnostic-export call site in the daemon.
